Generalized Symmetries Journal Club

This is a journal club that seeks to learn and discuss modern advancements in the understandings of so-called generalized symmetries or higher-form symmetries, and their consequences in condensed matter systems.

Format

We will meet in-person at MPI-PKS, and plan to stream the meetings on Zoom for external participants.

Schedule 2023/24

We plan to meet weekly or biweekly, depending on participation, tentatively on Fridays at 1:30 pm Dresden local time.
